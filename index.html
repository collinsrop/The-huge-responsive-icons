Summer Hoops.

Nearly all of us experienced this childhood, especially from Christian backgrounds. Every festive season was welcomed zealously. Every year, the drill was similar: new clothes and shoes, rare foodstuffs, and interaction with distant relatives well well-known as “watu wa Nairobi”. It was so enjoyable that completing the end-of-year examination back in school was a breakthrough but a major milestone was marked when there was an academic improvement as there would be reduced rejection upon requesting festive stuff. This festive season came with a lot of social events, be it weddings, initiation ceremonies, etc., All roads were heading in a common direction basically, to the respective ancestral backgrounds
